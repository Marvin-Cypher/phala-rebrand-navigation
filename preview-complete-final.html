Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trusted Execution Environment (TEE)?

TEE is a secure area inside a main processor that guarantees code and data protection. It provides hardware-level isolation, ensuring that sensitive operations are performed in a secure environment that cannot be accessed by the operating system, hypervisor, or other applications.

How does Phala compare to other cloud providers?

Unlike traditional cloud providers, Phala offers hardware-level security guarantees with real-time attestation. We provide verifiable security where you can cryptographically prove your AI is running securely, not just trusting our security claims.

What's the performance impact of TEE protection?

Our TEE implementation has less than 5% performance overhead compared to bare metal execution. For GPU-intensive AI workloads, the impact is even lower, typically under 3%, making it suitable for production deployments.

Is Phala compatible with existing AI frameworks?

Yes, Phala supports all major AI frameworks including PyTorch, TensorFlow, Hugging Face, and more. You can migrate existing models with minimal code changes, often just requiring containerization with Docker.

What compliance standards does Phala meet?

Phala is SOC 2 Type II certified, HIPAA ready, and GDPR compliant. We also support FedRAMP requirements for government deployments and provide detailed audit trails for enterprise compliance needs.

How do I get started with Phala?

Getting started is simple: sign up for Phala Cloud, choose a pre-configured AI model or upload your own, and deploy in minutes. We offer $500 in free credits for new users and comprehensive documentation to help you get running quickly.
